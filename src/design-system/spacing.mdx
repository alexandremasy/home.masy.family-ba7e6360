## `--nav-h` — the sticky bar

Currently <TokenValue token="nav-h" fallback="56px" />. Anything that sticks in a full-bleed page
must offset by this height, or it hides behind the bar. It mirrors the header in `_app.tsx` (h-14).

<Unstyled>
  <div className="overflow-hidden rounded-xl border border-border/60">
    <div className="flex items-center bg-card px-4 shadow-soft" style={{ height: "var(--nav-h)" }}>
      <span className="font-mono text-xs text-foreground">--nav-h</span>
    </div>
    <div className="bg-muted/40 p-4 text-xs text-muted-foreground">
      Content starts below the bar.
    </div>
  </div>
</Unstyled>

## `.grid-bento` — the dashboard mosaic

2 columns on mobile, 4 at ≥640px, 6 at ≥1024px, with fixed row heights on desktop and
`grid-auto-flow: row dense`. Resize the preview to watch it reflow.

Placement is never a card's business — `BentoItem` carries it. See **Layout → Bento Grid**.
