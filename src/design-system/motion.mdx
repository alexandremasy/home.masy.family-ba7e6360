## prefers-reduced-motion

`styles.css` only silences the decorative layers under
`@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` — the cursor follower, the living gradient, and the
repas glow. The `.anim-*` utilities above are **not** gated yet. That is a known gap, not a claim
this page makes.
